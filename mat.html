Mat Pilates

Discover the power of Mat Pilates in this dynamic, low-impact class designed to strengthen your core, improve flexibility, and increase overall body awareness. Through a series of controlled movements, you'll focus on proper alignment and posture while targeting key muscle groups. This class will empower you to achieve better strength and stability.
