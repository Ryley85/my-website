Private Pilates

Elevate your Pilates practice with our Private Pilates sessions at Embody Pilates. Tailored to meet your individual needs and goals, our one-on-one instruction provides the ultimate personalised workout experience, ensuring you achieve optimal results.

What is Private Pilates?

Experience Personalised Fitness with Private Pilates

Private Pilates offers customised sessions with a dedicated instructor, focusing solely on your unique fitness journey. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, private sessions are designed to address your specific needs, from rehabilitation to advanced technique refinement.

Building Strength & Flexibility

Benefits of Private Pilates

Personalised Attention:Receive individualised guidance and feedback from our expert instructors.

Customised Workouts:Tailor each session to your fitness level, goals, and any specific concerns or limitations.

Faster Progress:Achieve your goals more quickly with targeted, efficient workouts.

Flexibility:Schedule sessions at times that are convenient for you.

Enhanced Motivation:Stay committed and motivated with the support of a dedicated instructor.

Class Structure

Our Private Pilates sessions are meticulously crafted to ensure you get the most out of each workout. Each session typically includes:

Assessment & Progress Tracking

Initial evaluation to understand your fitness level, goals, and any specific needs, with regular assessments to monitor and adjust your program.

Warm-Up & Cool Down

Gentle movements to prepare your body for the workout, and stretching techniques to aid in recovery and flexibility.

Customised Exercises

Tailored exercises that target your specific goals, whether it's core strength, flexibility, or rehabilitation
