Reformer Pilates

Unleash your potential with our Reformer Pilates classes at Embody Pilates. Our expert instructors guide you through dynamic workouts designed to strengthen, tone, and rejuvenate your body. Experience the transformative effects of Reformer Pilates and take your fitness journey to new heights.

What is Reformer Pilates?

Discover the Power of Reformer Pilates

Reformer Pilates utilises a specialised piece of equipment called the Reformer, which adds resistance to traditional Pilates exercises. This unique approach enhances core stability, improves posture, and increases flexibility, making it an ideal workout for all fitness levels.

Benefits of Reformer Pilates

Transform your body and mind with our dynamic, full-body Reformer Pilates class.

Full-Body Workout: Target multiple muscle groups simultaneously for a comprehensive fitness routine.

Improved Posture:Strengthen your core and back muscles to support a healthier, more aligned posture.

Increased Flexibility: Stretch and lengthen your muscles, enhancing overall flexibility and range of motion.

Enhanced Strength:Build lean muscle mass and boost overall strength with controlled, resistance-based movements.

Injury Prevention:Strengthen your stabilising muscles and improve balance to reduce the risk of injury.
